[ FAQ ]

Questions we hear

What if we already have ideas about where to add AI?

Good. Bring them. We'll validate, challenge, or refine them. Most teams have 1-2 good instincts buried under 10 distractions. We help you find which is which.

Do you need access to our codebase or data?

No. We work from product access, stakeholder interviews, and existing documentation. If you have usage analytics or user research, that helps—but it's not required.

What if we're not sure AI is even right for our product?

That's a valid outcome. If we don't find strong opportunities, we'll tell you. You'll pay for honesty, not a forced recommendation.

Can this be done remotely?

Yes. 100% of our engagements are remote. Interviews via Zoom. Async collaboration via Notion or your preferred tools.

How is this different from hiring a consultant?

Consultants give you frameworks. We give you decisions. Our output isn't “consider these options”—it's “build this first, here's why, here's what it takes.”

What if our timeline is urgent?

We can compress to 7 business days for an additional 25% fee. Quality stays the same; we just prioritize your project.
